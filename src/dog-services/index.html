Dogs

We aim to tailor our home pet care package to your specific needs, so that you only pay for what you are getting. Even if you have more than one dog, we will only charge per visit or walk.

Home visits

Basic package £8 per visit. This includes:

  • Feeding / watering
  • Cleaning up
  • Short walk
  • Play and cuddle
  • Regular updates from your dog via text or email
  • Watering plants, post, bins on bin day, lights etc as required.

We understand that different dogs have different exercise requirements. Additional dog walking can be added at £5 per 30 mins.

Dog walking

We offer dog walking for £10 per hour (minimum one hour), with the option to increase the length of the walk for £5 per 30 mins. We will drive to your home and walk your dog in his familiar surroundings.

Contact us

Please contact us to discuss your specific requirements, or to arrange a free home visit to meet you and your dog!
